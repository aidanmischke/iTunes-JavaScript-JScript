Make sure you are closed out of the iTunes application.
Hold the Windows Key and press "R" to bring up the Run dialog box.
Type 
"C:\Program Files (x86)\iTunes\iTunes.exe" /setPrefInt allow-half-stars 1 
or
"E:\Program Files (x86)\iTunes\iTunes.exe" /setPrefInt allow-half-stars 1 
then press “Enter“. 


If that doesn’t work, try
C:\Program Files (x86)\iTunes\iTunes.exe” /setPrefInt allow-half-stars 1
